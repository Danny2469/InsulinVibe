Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your privacy is protected.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take your privacy seriously and use appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information. These include:
